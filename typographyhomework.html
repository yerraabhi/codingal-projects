CRICKET

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played by two teams of eleven players, where one team bats to score runs while the other bowls and fields to restrict them. Played on a large, typically oval field, a central pitch, the objective is to score more runs than the opposing team. The game involves batting, bowling, and fielding, with various formats and rules. Originated in England, cricket is particularly popular in countries like India, Australia, and Pakistan, often seen as the "gentleman's game".
